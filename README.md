# CO2-Emission
CO2 Emission per Country from Year 2000 to 2011.
This is a visualization project on Tableau for module 6 of Google Data Analytics Professional course, Share Data Through the art of visualization. It shows the CO2 emissions per capita for each country from seven regions, East Asia & Pacific, Europe & Central Asia, Latin American & Carribean, Middle East & North Africa, North America, South Asia and Sub Sahara Africa from 2000-2011.
